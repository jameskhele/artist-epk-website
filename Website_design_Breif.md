## 3. Full Bio
Shevyn Roberts is a Houston, Texas–born award-winning artist whose career spans music, dance, and acting. A true triple threat, she is a six-time National Solo Dance Champion, shaping her powerful stage presence and audience connection.

She first gained attention with her debut EP *Superstitious* and is a Hollywood Music in Media Award winner for Best Pop Song and multi-time nominee.

She has shared stages with Justin Bieber and performed at large-scale events with strong audience engagement.

She has appeared in television and film including *Days of Our Lives* and *Dead Ant*.

She is also the founder of Shevyn’s Dance Academy, a nonprofit supporting young performers.

Now transitioning into country music, her work focuses on storytelling, authenticity, and Texas-rooted identity.
