## 2. Artist Bio

### Short Bio
Shevyn Roberts is an award-winning singer, songwriter, actress, and six-time National Solo Dance Champion from Houston, Texas. A Hollywood Music in Media Award winner for Best Pop Song and multi-time nominee, she has also been featured as one of Music Connection Magazine’s “Hot 100 Unsigned Artists & Bands.” She has performed on major stages, including opening for Justin Bieber. Known for her dynamic stage presence and powerful storytelling, Shevyn delivers emotionally engaging performances that connect deeply with audiences.

### Full Bio
Shevyn Roberts is a Houston, Texas–born award-winning artist whose career spans music, dance, and acting. A six-time National Solo Dance Champion, she developed a commanding stage presence and deep audience connection early on.

She gained national attention with her debut EP *Superstitious* and is a Hollywood Music in Media Award winner for Best Pop Song and multi-time nominee.

She has shared stages with globally recognized artists including Justin Bieber and performed at large-scale events, delivering high-energy and emotionally compelling performances.

She has appeared in television and film, including *Days of Our Lives* and *Dead Ant*.

She is the founder of Shevyn’s Dance Academy, a nonprofit mentoring young performers.

Now transitioning into country music, she embraces her Texas roots with storytelling-driven music that blends authenticity with commercial appeal.
